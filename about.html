About us



Late Manik Bhattacharjee, a full time devoted social worker of this state and a true soldier of the Bharat Sevashram Sangha, was an Educationist who was closely associated with many institutions and feeling the necessities of the Dimapurians he along with his close associates Late H.P.Sarkar , Late K.P. Nandy, Late Gobinda Chandra Paira particularly and others in general esablished The Pranab Vidyapith H.S.S(Sc. & Comm.). Pranabananda Women’s College, at Dimapur and a boarding School named Pranab Vidyapith at Bokajan. He with his co-workers and well-wishers initiated the materilisation of the relief activities, medical services and other welfare activities of the Sangha.Upto 2000 all these welfare activities were carried out under the banner of Dimapur Hindu Milan Mandir. In the year 2000, the Dimapur Hindu Milan Mandir which was an affiliated branch of the Bharat Sevashram Sangha, was transited to Bharat Sevashrsm Sangha, Dimapur Branch. 17th January, 1984 was a red letter day in the history of Pranab Vidyapith. It was on that auspicious day of Pous Purnima in 1984, the Vidyapith came to its existance. The School starts its humble beginning under the holy banner of Hindu Milan Mandir with 145 students on roll and with 6 teachers in the present Mandir Premises. Later on in 1985 it was shifted to the present site in a Thatched house. The Vidyapith was recognised by Nagaland Board of School Education, Kohima in 1986 for its excellent results in its maiden appearance in H.S.L.C. Examination in 1985.


The school has been shaped into a multistoreyed building where 2647 students are receiving education today. It is a co-education centre with classes Nursery to 12. In the year 2000 the Vidyapith was up graded to Higher Secondary (Science) stream. In 2005 the Management has added one more feather to its cap by introducing Commerce stream to meet the growing demand of the parents and guardians. ​


We have introduced coaching for JEE and NEET for Science students and Common Profeciency Test (CPT) for commerce students.

  • Pranab Jyoti Award for Toppers
  • Well equipped Science Laboratories & Computer Lab
  • Experience & Dedicated Teachers
  • Co-curricular Activities
  • Special & Remedial classes.
