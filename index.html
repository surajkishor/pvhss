Our Mission

The Holy Acharya Srimat Swami Pranabanandajee maharaj emphasised upon practising religion in its truest form. He did not believe in dogma but wished that all must have the rea education to follow the actual teaching of their religion whatever they might be. The Vidyapith intends to impart education to the young students, keeping in view the object to ‘man making and character building’


Our Motto

The Vidyapith encourages the students to develop in all aspects of human life, physical, moral & intellectual. The Vidyapith has opened the Higher Secondary Science stream in year 2000 to make the student eligible to professional studies viz. Engineering, Medical and other higher technical studies. From the academic year i.e. 2005-2006 Pranab Vidyapith has introduced Commerce stream.


Our Vision

The Vidyapith gives importance in participation of the students in various activities of social and spiritual orientation viz. joining and arranging relief activities for people in distress, organising seminars on literacy , medical aid programme, cultural and religious issues etc.
