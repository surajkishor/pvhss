Withdrawal of Student


  • Atleast 15 days notice in writing on a prescribed form is to be given duly signed by the guardian/ parent before withdrawing a student from the school.

  • T.C. is ordinarily issued within 7 days of application on clearance of all dues of the school

  • No student who fails in two consecutive years in succession will be retained in the school

  • The school reserves the right to ask the parents to withdraw his/ her ward (a) if their progress in studies is unsatisfactory. (b)If their attendence is irregular. (c) if their conduct is detrimental to general moral tone and sanctity of the school.

  • One month tuition fees is charged for Transfer Certificate along with Term fees. No term fees will be charged if the application for T.C. is submitted before 31st December.
